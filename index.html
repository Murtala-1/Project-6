Turn Based Game

Openclassroom Project 6

Created By: Adewale Muritala Akinyemi

  • Each player can move 3 spaces horizontally or vertically or less than that.
  • If you feel healthy, you can find a player on the game board to fight with.
  • The damage depends on the player's weapon
  • Choose another weapon will relace the weapon you had before
  • The player can choose to attack or defend against the next shot
  • If the player chooses to defend themselves, they sustain 50% less damage than normal
